About PETALS INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

At PETALS INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL we combine rigorous academics with a supportive culture. Each student receives individual attention, clear expectations, and the space to develop at their own pace—in studies, character, and life skills.

Our Mission

We aim to build thoughtful, capable, and caring young minds through clear teaching, strong ethics, and real-world connections—so students excel in examinations and in life.

Our Vision

To stand as Bhubaneswar’s choice for disciplined yet vibrant learning, where pupils are prepared for an evolving world with both knowledge and character.

Our Values

Integrity, accountability, kindness, and respect shape our daily conduct—in class, on the field, and in all dealings with families and the wider community.
